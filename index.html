7 STEP PROCESS

Step
01

Request a Quote online

Once you have an idea of what you would like for your space, the first thing you need to do is request a quote on our website. www.usbooths.com/quotes

Step
02

Approve The Quote

After the information has been reviewed, our team will either ask for additional information or send you a final quote through email for you to revise and approve.

Step
03

Invoice

Upon quote approval, the system will automatically send an invoice with a 50% fee for you to pay as a security deposit.

Step
04

Swatch

When payment has been received we start the process by demonstrating a variety of swatches that you can choose from.

Step
05

3D Design/Virtual Reality

We use on point technology to create hyper-realistic renders in which you are able to pre-visualize your setting with the design giving you the opportunity to envision it and modify.

06

Manufacturing Process

07

Order Completion
