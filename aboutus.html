About Us

After the outbreak of the pandemic, Mayo had to shift his working style. Now, he designs 3D furniture in software, creates files in CNC woodworking machines, and hires CNC businesses on an hourly basis to create stunning and affordable furniture pieces.

Today, Mayo has a big team of furniture and upholstery experts who work together to create perfect masterpieces and influence the community. Premium quality, swift service and originality is the hallmark of his extraordinary work.

Mayo envisions scaling his network and outspread his customer base across the USA while managing the entire furniture manufacturing from his garage. He wants to lead the market by providing high-end furniture at the best price and unmatched quality. He pays special emphasis on logistics so that customers enjoy a flawless experience.

Mayo’s prowess of transforming people’s illusions into reality is unparalleled. He is a pro at creating vintage and modern furniture and upholstery designs. His exemplary work includes The Urban Lounge Las Vegas, Culinary Drop Out, and Hellen’s Restaurant.
Mayo’s team of brilliant designers and workers puts in the best efforts to achieve 100% customer satisfaction. His every furniture piece embody comfort, elegance and worth elegance and worth.
Mayo has successfully served the restaurant industry and won great recognition. Eyeing the future, he carries out detailed research to create fascinating and space-age designs to match the demands of the newfound era. You will love Mayo’s artisans’ work due to the awesome designs, perfect measurement, intricate finishing, top-notch material, compact designing and much more.
